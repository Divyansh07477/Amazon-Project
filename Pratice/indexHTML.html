Black Hat Hackers: These are individuals who engage in illegal activities, using their skills to exploit vulnerabilities for personal gain, causing harm, or stealing sensitive information. White Hat Hackers: Also known as ethical hackers, these individuals use their skills to help organizations improve their security by identifying vulnerabilities before malicious hackers can exploit them. Grey Hat Hackers: These hackers fall somewhere in between. They may sometimes break the law but do so without malicious intent, often finding vulnerabilities and reporting them, sometimes for a fee or recognition.
